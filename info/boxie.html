Boxiehouse Logo

I created this logo for an ultimate frisbee fall league team named Boxiehouse. The request was to create a colorful logo that features birdhouses. I illustrated the logo in Procreate.

Logo progress

In an early alternative version, I was inspired by the Waffle House logo to make a parody version for Boxiehouse. The team voted and chose the bird house version over the Waffle House.
