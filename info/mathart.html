Carleton College Math + Art Symposium

Print Design

Procreate - Adobe InDesign

I was asked to create a logo, poster, and digital banner for Carleton College's Math + Art Symposium titled Points, Lines, Angles, & Shapes.

Following the brand guidelines of Carleton College, I created a design that celebrates the combination of art and mathematics.

The brief asked for the incorporation of a pattern inspired by historic geometric tiling. The pattern I designed is made up of penrose tiles and the kite shape that makes up an aperiodic monotile, commonly known as "the hat."
